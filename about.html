About CareerSteps

Guiding students towards the right career path with clarity & confidence

Our Mission

CareerSteps was founded in 2025 with a simple belief: No student should feel confused or forced while choosing their career.

In India, millions of students choose Science, Commerce, or Arts due to peer pressure, lack of guidance, or limited information. CareerSteps exists to change that reality by providing clear, unbiased, and student-friendly career guidance.

Why We Exist

To bridge the gap between traditional education systems and modern career opportunities such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Digital Marketing, Design, and emerging technologies.

Our Approach

Our Career Compass is designed using structured questions that analyze interests, strengths, and work preferences to suggest suitable career streams and options.

Who We Help

  • Students after 10th choosing between Science, Commerce & Arts
  • 12th pass students exploring degree & career options
  • Students preparing for competitive exams and professional courses
  • Parents seeking reliable career information

Important Disclaimer

CareerSteps provides guidance for educational and informational purposes only. Final career decisions should be taken by students and parents based on personal interests, financial situation, and professional counseling where required.
